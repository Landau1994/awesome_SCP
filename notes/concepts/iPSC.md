# iPSC

## Summary

iPSC stands for induced pluripotent stem cell, a reprogrammed cell type with pluripotent potential.

## Notes

- Common source for differentiation and disease modeling studies.
- Frequently used in cardiomyocyte and neural lineage SCP studies.

## References

- Foundational reprogramming literature (Yamanaka factors).
