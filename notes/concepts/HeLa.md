# HeLa

## Summary

HeLa is a widely used human cervical cancer cell line and a common benchmark matrix in proteomics workflow optimization.

## Notes

- Frequently used for LC-MS method tuning and software benchmarking.
- Suitable for reproducibility and sensitivity comparison experiments.

## References

- Originates from cervical carcinoma cell lineage (historical standard model).
