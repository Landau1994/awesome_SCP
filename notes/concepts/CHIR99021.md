# CHIR99021

## Summary

CHIR99021 is a selective GSK3 inhibitor widely used in stem-cell culture and differentiation protocols.

## Notes

- Frequently used in reprogramming and lineage-specification conditions.
- May influence Wnt/beta-catenin-associated signaling programs.

## References
