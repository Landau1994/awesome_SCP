# Network biology

## Summary

Network biology studies biological systems through interaction networks among genes, proteins, and pathways.

## Notes

- Useful for systems-level interpretation of proteomics signatures.
- Often integrates interaction databases and enrichment analyses.

## References

- Systems biology framework for molecular interaction analysis.
