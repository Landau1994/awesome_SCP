# Bortezomib

## Summary

Bortezomib is a proteasome inhibitor used in cancer therapy and often used in perturbation studies.

## Notes

- Commonly used to modulate protein degradation pathways.
- Can induce broad proteostasis and stress-response signatures.

## References

- Approved therapeutic in multiple myeloma contexts.
