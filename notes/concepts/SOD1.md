# SOD1

## Summary

SOD1 encodes superoxide dismutase 1, an antioxidant enzyme involved in reactive oxygen species detoxification.

## Notes

- Frequently implicated in stress-response and stem-cell biology contexts.
- May show discordance between mRNA and protein-level behavior.

## References

- Core oxidative-stress pathway component.
