# minProb

## Summary

minProb is a left-censored missing-value imputation strategy often used in quantitative proteomics.

## Notes

- Imputes missing values from a low-intensity distribution tail.
- Commonly applied before differential abundance analysis.

## References

- Frequently used in Perseus-like workflows.
