# Saccharomyces cerevisiae

## Summary

Saccharomyces cerevisiae is budding yeast, a major model organism in cell biology and proteomics.

## Notes

- Extensively used for systems biology and aging studies.
- Offers strong genetics and controlled perturbation frameworks.

## References

- Canonical eukaryotic model organism.
