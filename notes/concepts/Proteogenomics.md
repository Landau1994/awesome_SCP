# Proteogenomics

## Summary

Proteogenomics integrates proteomics with genomics/transcriptomics to connect sequence variation and protein-level phenotypes.

## Notes

- Used to identify proteomic consequences of genomic alterations.
- Common in cancer and biomarker discovery studies.

## References

- Established multi-omics integration paradigm.
