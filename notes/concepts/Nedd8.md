# Nedd8

## Summary

Nedd8 is a ubiquitin-like modifier involved in protein neddylation and proteostasis regulation.

## Notes

- Neddylation influences protein stability and signaling.
- Relevant in stress response and disease-associated proteome remodeling.

## References

- Ubiquitin-like modification pathway component.
