# Cycloheximide

## Summary

Cycloheximide is a translation inhibitor commonly used to study protein synthesis and turnover dynamics.

## Notes

- Blocks elongation on eukaryotic ribosomes.
- Useful in pulse-chase and degradation/translation-rate experiments.

## References

- Standard inhibitor in translational regulation studies.
