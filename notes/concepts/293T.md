# 293T

## Summary

293T (HEK293T) is a human embryonic kidney-derived cell line widely used in molecular biology and proteomics method development.

## Notes

- Commonly used as a robust and easy-to-culture benchmark sample.
- Often appears in spike-in, optimization, and validation experiments.
