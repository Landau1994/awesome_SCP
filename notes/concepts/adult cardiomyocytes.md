# adult cardiomyocytes

## Summary

Adult cardiomyocytes are mature heart muscle cells with specialized contractile and metabolic states.

## Notes

- Frequently used as a comparison baseline for iPSC-derived cardiomyocytes.
- Relevant to developmental trajectory and disease modeling analyses.
