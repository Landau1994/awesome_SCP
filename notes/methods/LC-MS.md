# LC-MS

**Liquid chromatography-mass spectrometry workflow for separation and molecular detection.**

## Overview

LC-MS combines chromatographic separation with mass analysis and is a foundational platform for proteomics profiling.

## Key Features

- **Separation + Detection**: Resolves complex peptide mixtures before MS.
- **Broad Applicability**: Used in discovery and targeted workflows.
- **Instrument Flexibility**: Compatible with multiple MS platforms.

## Workflow

```text
Sample prep → LC separation → Ionization → MS acquisition → Data analysis
```

## Performance

- **Proteins per cell**: Method- and platform-dependent
- **Throughput**: Moderate to high
- **Data completeness**: Improved with optimized acquisition/software

## Advantages

- Mature and robust analytical framework
- High sensitivity with nano-flow configurations
- Scalable from bulk to low-input samples

## Limitations

- Requires method and instrument optimization
- Sensitive to column and sample quality
- Trade-off between depth and speed
